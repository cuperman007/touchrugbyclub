The simplest way to create a touch rugby club website.

Our mission

It is our mission is to provide the guidance and tools for touch rugby clubs to create a website for the purposes of player recruitment and retention.

Website advice

Your website should be practical and useful. It needs to quickly provide your visitors with the information that they are looking for.

These are our top tips for creating useful content on your club website.

Training

Where and when do you train? What should players expect from training?

Top tip: Make sure someone is available to welcome new players and introduce them around.

Fees

Is there a membership cost? Do players pay per session?

Top tip: Offer a free trial for 1 or 2 weeks to encourage new players.

Contact info

How can people contact you? Do you have any social media accounts?

Top tip: Create social media accounts and post regular updates

Celebrations

Celebrate the achievements of your players, coaches and referees.

Top tip: Link to affiliate organisation like England Touch or your regional franchise.

FAQs

How much will this cost?

We know that all touch rugby clubs are run by volunteers with very low budgets so the Touch Rugby Network helps you to get started for free.

If you need technical support or a leg up to get started then may be a charge, but rest assured there are no hidden costs, we will let you know about any charges before you incur them.

See our pricing section for details

Can I edit my own content?

Yes you can, but you'll need to know HTML, CSS and GIT.

Most local sports team websites don't require regular updates, so to keep costs low we use static website hosting on free servers.

Where is the website hosted and are there any on-going costs?

The websites are published to GitHub repositories and hosted using GitHub pages, a free offering from the GitHub organisation. The websites are hosted on shared servers around the world.

Do I need to concern myself with GDPR?

No personally identifiable information is captured

Do I need to concern myself with cookies and tracking policies?

The Touch Rugby Network does not capture any cookies on any of our websites, but we do use google analytics to check on viewer analytics and statistics.

Can I customise the content, or include widgets and scripts?

Usually yes - Provided we can host your custom content / widgets within a static website. Get in touch to let us know the details and we'll let you know if it can be done.
